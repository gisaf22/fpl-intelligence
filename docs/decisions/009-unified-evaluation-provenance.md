# ADR-009 — Unified Evaluation Provenance

**Status:** Accepted — Phases A–D merged (2026-06-05)
**Date:** 2026-06-05
**Applies to:** `research/foundation/`, `research/families/`, `model/governance/evaluation_metadata.yaml`, `model/assemble/composition_study.py`, `domain/registry/{governance_lookup,governance_types}.py`, `serve/scoring/signal_selector.py`
**ADLC source:** [adlc.md §2 (validate stage emits verdicts)](../architecture/adlc.md), [system-model.md (measurement plane)](../architecture/system-model.md)

---

## Context

Signal **evaluation** — deciding whether a stat is trustworthy enough to influence recommendations —
is currently fragmented across **three producers writing two incompatible verdict vocabularies
through three different provenance mechanisms**, stitched together by a hardcoded allowlist.

**Three producers:**
1. `research/foundation/` (EDA 0–7) — population-level *characterization* of all signals → registry CSV.
2. `research/families/*/validate/` — per-family lens *validation* of engineered signals → CSVs in `research/runs/`.
3. `model/assemble/composition_study.py` — partial-rho / bootstrap-CI / FDR *composition evaluation* → `synth01_decisions.yaml`.

**Two verdict vocabularies (complementary, not competing):**
- Foundation: `downstream_status` ∈ {eligible, caveated, blocked} + `promotion_class`.
- Lens/synth: `lifecycle_state` ∈ {candidate, approved, excluded, not_applicable} + `leakage_risk`.

**Three provenance mechanisms — and the core defect:**
- Registry CSV: auto-generated by EDA, enriched at promote (`model/governance/`). Good.
- `evaluation_metadata.yaml`: **hand-authored.** Lens studies emit only CSVs; a human manually
  transcribes verdicts into the YAML, and manually merges `synth01_decisions.yaml` back in. **No code
  writes this file** (confirmed: no YAML writer exists for it).
- `synth01_decisions.yaml`: auto, but produced **inside `model/assemble`** — i.e. research-grade evidence
  generated in the model layer (a `research → model` boundary smudge).

**The bridge:** because the two tiers are never reconciled, the scorer leans on
`PRE_LENS_SIGNAL_ALLOWLIST` — a hardcoded `frozenset` of 14 raw signals (`goals_scored`, `xg`, `assists`…)
that have a foundation verdict but no lens record — to keep them from being rejected as "ungoverned"
at scoring time. The allowlist is governance *content* (a decision) living in `domain` code.

**The problem is provenance, not schema.** The two vocabularies measure different things by design
(characterize vs validate); they are complementary stages, not duplicate truth sources. What is missing
is (a) an automated path from study → verdict, and (b) a reconciliation so every scored signal has one
uniform record. See the full assessment for Parts 1–7.

## Decision

Normalize and automate evaluation **outputs and provenance**. Do **not** add a layer, merge the
methodological tiers, or collapse the existing `DAL → research → model → serve` (+ `domain` leaf) split.

### 1. Verdict vocabulary reconciliation (the one judgment call — LOCKED)

A signal that has only a foundation `downstream_status` (no lens study) is mapped to a `lifecycle_state`
under a **cautious-grade** policy: nothing is ever auto-`approved` without a real study, but usable
signals are not benched.

| Foundation `downstream_status` | → `lifecycle_state` | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| `eligible` | `candidate` | passed the screen, but provisional — only a lens study earns `approved` |
| `caveated` | `candidate` | use-with-care = provisional; still scores, flagged not-approved |
| `blocked` | `excluded` | clearly unusable |

This encodes "we do not assume validity": no raw signal is `approved` without a lens study. Whether
`caveated` signals should later be *benched* (i.e. `caveated → excluded`) is deferred to its own
reviewed decision — it is a behavior change, this is not.

**Blast-radius proof (read-only, against `eda_03_joint_registry.csv`):** of the 14 raw signals,
**44 (signal, position) pairs score today** via the allowlist; under this mapping **44 score after** —
**0 flips.** Every confirmed raw pair maps to `candidate` (still scores); the only `→ excluded` cases
are `blocked` pairs that already were not scoring. Deleting the allowlist under this mapping is
**provably behavior-preserving.**

### 2. Provenance: every verdict is machine-emitted from a study

`evaluation_metadata.yaml` (and any successor) must be **generated**, not hand-authored. Lens studies
emit a machine-readable verdict record with a traceable pointer to their evidence run. The synth merge
is automated, not hand-copied.

### 3. Composition uses `research.kernels` for its statistical primitives

`model/assemble/composition_study.py` currently reimplements research-grade statistics inline
(partial Spearman rho via `lstsq`, bootstrap CIs, permutation baseline, FDR moderation). The study
**stays in `model/assemble`** — weight derivation is legitimately a composition job — but its
statistical *primitives* move into `research.kernels` (which `model/assemble` is already permitted to
import; the import-linter exempts kernels). This puts the methodology where it is reusable and tested,
without relocating the study or inventing a one-off single-consumer research artifact.

This is a deliberately *light* fix: the prior model/assemble audit flagged the inline stats as the one
"model recomputes research evidence" smell, but the evidence here is composition-specific and
single-consumer, so a full relocation would over-reach and is tangential to this ADR's provenance goal.

### 4. One normalized read model in `domain/registry`

The scoring **decision** path consumes a single uniform verdict per (signal, position) via
`domain/registry/verdict.py:resolve_verdict() → SignalVerdict`, which merges the foundation registry row
(promotion_class, layer_role, rho, downstream_status) with the lens/derived governance verdict
(`resolve_governance`). The scorer reads no raw registry columns — every confirm/caveat decision reads
the one normalized shape (preserving `serve ↛ model`). `PRE_LENS_SIGNAL_ALLOWLIST` is deleted (Phase A).

**Scope:** this unifies the *verdict-decision* path (scoring). `serve/reporting` reads registry columns
(`promotion_class`/`downstream_status`) for **descriptive** display — counts, grouping, version diffs —
and only ever sees the single foundation tier (never the lens YAML), so it carries no two-vocabulary fork
and is deliberately out of scope: bulk aggregation does not route through a per-signal verdict accessor.

## Migration path (phased; each phase independently shippable + green)

**Phase A — make the implicit explicit (safe, behavior-preserving):** derive explicit `candidate`/
`excluded` records for the 14 raw signals from their foundation `downstream_status` using the locked
mapping; delete `PRE_LENS_SIGNAL_ALLOWLIST`. Verified no-op by the blast-radius proof above.

**Phase B — normalized read model:** the scoring decision path reads one `SignalVerdict`
(`domain/registry/verdict.py:resolve_verdict`) merging foundation columns + lens/derived verdict; the
scorer reads no raw registry columns. Reporting's descriptive registry reads are out of scope (§4).

**Phase C — automate provenance (merged):** lens studies emit a machine-readable evidence record
(`research/families/*/validate/evidence.yaml`, written by `study.run()`) beside a hand-authored
judgment record (`annotations.yaml`); `model/governance/generate_evaluation_metadata.py` merges
the two tiers with `synth01_decisions.yaml` to generate `evaluation_metadata.yaml` (banner-marked
generated, drift-guarded by `tests/test_generate_evaluation_metadata.py`). Seeded by a one-time
lossless backfill of the prior hand-authored YAML (`_backfill_verdict_records.py`); re-running the
studies to refresh evidence is a research process (deferred). Hand-authoring retired.

**Phase D — kernels for composition stats (merged):** the statistical primitives moved into
`research.kernels` — `partial_spearman` + `bootstrap_partial_rho` (`redundancy.py`),
`permutation_rho_baseline` (`resampling.py`), `fraction_rank_order_changed` (`stability.py`),
each unit-tested — and `composition_study.py` imports them (kernels are import-linter exempt for
`model`). The study stays in `model/assemble` and continues to own weight derivation
(`_normalize_weights`, `_bootstrap_weights`, `_composite_rho`). Extraction verified
behavior-preserving by numeric equivalence against the prior inline implementations.

Phases A–B are mechanical and low-risk. C is larger (touches the research pipeline); D is an isolated
refactor independent of A–C.

## Consequences

- **Positive:** one auditable provenance chain study → verdict → registry → scorer; the hardcoded
  allowlist is gone; `domain` holds zero governance *content* (only the reader + types); composition
  statistics share the tested `research.kernels` methodology instead of an inline reimplementation;
  `serve` reads one uniform verdict.
- **Deferred (separate decisions):** whether `caveated` signals are benched pending studies; running
  genuine lens studies for raw signals that warrant real verdicts (a research process, not a refactor).
- **Unchanged:** `serve ↛ model`, the path-based lifecycle gate as the only operational path, and the
  foundation-vs-lens methodological distinction.

## Open decisions

None blocking. The `downstream_status → lifecycle_state` mapping is locked (cautious-grade, §1). The
`caveated → excluded` benching question is explicitly deferred.
