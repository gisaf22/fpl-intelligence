# describe-60min-boundary

**Stage:** explore · **Mode:** descriptive · **Verdict:** accepted · **Date:** 2026-05-22
**Evidence:** `research/foundation/population/population_boundary.ipynb` — notebook retired; descriptive finding preserved in the informative population layer

Describes a regime shift in the points distribution across the 60-minute line — the
boundary that motivated the population filter. This is **descriptive only**: it records
*that* the distribution shifts, not that minutes *cause* points. "An extra 30 minutes
causes +X points" would be causal (Pearl rung 2) and is deliberately out of scope for
this project. The mode tag exists precisely to keep that line uncrossed.
